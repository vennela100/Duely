# Privacy Policy — Duely

**Last updated: 14 June 2026**

Duely ("the app") is a lending-ledger app for small lenders to track customers, loans, and daily repayments. This policy explains what data the app handles and how.

## Who controls your data
You (the lender using the app) control all data you enter. The app developer does not access, sell, or share your customers' information.

## What data the app handles
- **Customer & loan records** you enter: names, phone numbers, optional address, deal/loan amounts, and repayment history.
- **Contacts** (optional): if you choose to import a customer from your phone's contacts, the app reads contact name and number **only at that moment, only for the contact you pick**. The app does not upload or store your full contacts list.
- **SMS** (optional): when you record a repayment and choose to send a receipt, the app sends a payment-confirmation SMS from your phone's SIM to that customer. The app **does not read, collect, or store** any SMS.

## Where your data is stored
1. **On your device** — the primary copy, stored locally on your phone.
2. **Cloud sync (optional)** — if you sign in, your data is backed up to **Google Firebase (Firestore)** under your own private account space, so you can recover it on a new phone. Only you (signed in with your email) can access it.
3. **Google Drive (optional)** — if you connect Drive, a backup copy is saved to a "Duely Backups" folder in **your own Google Drive**. The app uses the `drive.file` scope, meaning it can only see files it created — it cannot read your other Drive files.

## What we do NOT do
- We do **not** sell or share your data with third parties.
- We do **not** read your messages or your full contacts list.
- We do **not** use your data for advertising.

## Permissions and why
- **Contacts** — to import a customer's name and number (only when you choose to).
- **Send SMS** — to send payment-confirmation receipts to your customers from your SIM.
- **Internet** — for optional cloud backup/sync.

## Data retention and deletion
- Local data stays on your device until you clear it or uninstall the app.
- Cloud data stays in your Firebase/Drive account until you delete it. You can clear all data in the app (Profile → Data & backup → Clear all data), disconnect cloud, or delete the backups from your Google account.

## Children
Duely is not intended for children under 13.
